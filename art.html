I'm just getting started with acrylic pouring—an experiment that's as much about fun as it is about art. Growing up, my parents always pegged me as the logical one and my sister as the creative spirit, but after a couple years of therapy I figured I can also paint wow. I mostly paint for friends, and I even did a few commissions for Motol Hospital.

If you're curious about my work or want to chat about a commission, feel free to reach out: matous.eibich@seznam.cz
